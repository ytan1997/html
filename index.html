The Art of War

Sun Tzu

'The wise warrior avoids the battle.'

A classic book of military strategies, Sun Izu's The Art of War is about resolving conflicts and achieving peace. Often read as a book of war tactics, it is much more than that: it does not glorify war but simply presents the best ways to win one that has already been waged. Despite being rooted in military philosophy and wisdom, the relevance of Sun Tzu's work is not restricted in scope and time; its lessons about conflict, strategy and leadership remain applicable across ages, in all areas of life.
